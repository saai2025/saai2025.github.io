Accommodation

Hostels: Bonn has several hostels that provide cost-effective lodging options for travelers. Youth Hostel Bonn is among the most affordable options.

Budget Hotels: There are budget-friendly hotel options in Bonn, such as B&B Hotel Bonn, and Dorint Venusberg Bonn. These offer comfortable and affordable stays.

Short-Term Rentals: Consider booking short-term apartment rentals through platforms like Airbnb, where you can often find economical accommodations with kitchen facilities for added convenience.

Venue

The Summer School will take place at heart of the University of Bonn. This venue offers state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped lecture halls, modern classrooms, and comfortable spaces for workshops and group activities.

Location and Transportation

  • From Cologne/Bonn Airport (CGN) to Venusberg Campus, Bonn: You can take a taxi from the airport to the campus, which is a convenient option. Alternatively, the bus SB60 connects the airport to Bonn central station, from where you can use local transportation, e.g., Bus 601/602, to reach Venusberg Campus.

  • From Frankfurt Airport (FRA) to Venusberg Campus, Bonn: The most efficient option is to take a high-speed train (ICE or IC) from Frankfurt Airport to Bonn Central Station. From there, local transportation, e.g., Bus 601/602, will take you to Venusberg Campus.

  • From Dusseldorf Airport (DUS) to Venusberg Campus, Bonn: You can take a direct train (RE5) from Dusseldorf Airport to Bonn Central Station. Once you arrive, local transportation options are available for the short journey to the campus.

Local Transportation in NRW:

North Rhine-Westphalia (NRW) has an efficient public transportation system. You can use buses and trams to navigate the city of Bonn and the surrounding region. Consider purchasing the WochenTicket, which allows unlimited travel on regional trains and local public transport for a week in certain circles in NRW.
